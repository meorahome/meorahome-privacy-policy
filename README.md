# Privacy Policy for Meora Home Manager

**Last updated: February 16, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes the policies and procedures regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when using the Meora Home Content Manager application ("the App") and informs You about Your privacy rights and how the law protects You.

## Interpretation and Definitions

### Interpretation

Words with capitalized initial letters have meanings defined under the following conditions. These definitions apply regardless of whether they appear in singular or plural form.

### Definitions

- **App (Application):** Refers to Meora Home Manager, a personal content management tool used for managing and publishing content related to the Meora Home Etsy shop.
- **Operator:** Refers to Meora Home (referred to as "We", "Us", or "Our" in this Policy), the sole operator and user of the App.
- **Third-Party Services:** External platforms and APIs integrated with the App, including Google Drive, Google Sheets, and Pinterest API.
- **Personal Data:** Any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.
- **Processing:** Any operation performed on data, including collection, storage, modification, transfer, or deletion.
- **You/User:** Any individual whose data may be processed through the App's operations, including visitors to content published via the App.

## Purpose of the App

Meora Home Manager is a personal content management tool operated solely by Meora Home. The App assists the Operator in managing content workflows for the Meora Home Etsy shop, including:

- Organizing and accessing product images stored in Google Drive
- Managing product listing data via Google Sheets
- Preparing and scheduling content for Pinterest under the Operator's manual review and approval

The App serves as a productivity tool that streamlines content preparation. All content is reviewed and approved by the Operator before publishing. The App is not publicly available and does not provide services to external users.

## Data Collection and Processing

### Data Processed Through Third-Party Services

The App interacts with the following third-party services through their official APIs:

- **Google Drive:** Used to access and retrieve product images stored in the Operator's own Google Drive account.
- **Google Sheets:** Used to read and manage product listing data maintained by the Operator.
- **Pinterest API:** Used to create and publish pins to the Operator's Pinterest business account, subject to the Operator's review and approval.

### Types of Data Processed

The App may process the following types of data:

- Product images and descriptions from the Operator's own accounts
- Product listing metadata (titles, tags, prices) from the Operator's Google Sheets
- Pinterest account data necessary for content publishing

### Data Not Collected

The App does **not**:

- Collect personal data from end users or website visitors
- Use cookies or tracking technologies
- Collect usage data, IP addresses, or device information from third parties
- Store or process data of children or any individuals under the age of 13
- Require user registration or account creation by external users

## Data Storage and Retention

The App utilizes n8n as a workflow management platform to assist the Operator with content preparation tasks. The App does not maintain a separate database or persistent data storage. Data handled by the App resides within the Operator's existing accounts on Google Drive, Google Sheets, and Pinterest.

Temporary data processed during workflow execution is not retained after the workflow completes.

We take reasonable measures to protect data processed through the App, including:

- Using official APIs with proper authentication (OAuth 2.0) for all third-party service connections
- Limiting access to the App to the Operator only
- Not sharing processed data with any unauthorized third parties
