## Purpose of the App

Meora Home Manager is a personal content management tool operated solely by Meora Home. The App assists the Operator in preparing and managing content for the Meora Home Etsy shop Pinterest account.

The App's workflow includes:

- Organizing and accessing product images stored in the Operator's Google Drive
- Managing product listing data via the Operator's Google Sheets
- Preparing content drafts (pin descriptions, titles, and images) for Pinterest

**The Operator manually reviews, selects, and approves each individual pin before it is published to Pinterest.** No content is published without the Operator's explicit action and approval. The App does not perform any bulk or unsupervised publishing.

The App is not publicly available, has no end users, and does not provide services to external parties. It is used exclusively by the Operator for managing their own Pinterest business account.

## Data Collection and Processing
